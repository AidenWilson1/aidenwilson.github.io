The Bahamas is one of the worlds best sailing venues in the world. There are many protected harbours like Monatgue Bay and Elizabeth harbour that offer pristine sailing conditions and gorgoeus blue water.

Why is sailing in The Bahamas so good?

The Bahamas is a very god sailing venue for, you guessed it our, wind. Since the archipelago is located in the Atlantic, we experience very consistent wind. The Bahamas is under the influence of the north-east trade winds with average wind speeds of 14.5 knots.
